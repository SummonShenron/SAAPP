TRANSFORMATION MECHANICS:
The Super Saiyan transformation is an advanced genetic state achieved by powerful members of the Saiyan race. It relies on a high concentration of microscopic "S-Cells" within the individual's bloodstream, typically triggered by an intense emotional catalyst such as extreme rage or despair.

HISTORICAL TIMELINE RECAP:
- Planet Vegeta: The original homeworld of the Saiyan race, completely destroyed by the galactic tyrant Frieza to prevent the realization of the legendary Super Saiyan prophecy.
- The Namekian Conflict: Goku achieves the historic Super Saiyan form for the first time during a ferocious battle against Frieza on a self-destructing Planet Namek.
- The Android Menace: Future Trunks travels back in time utilizing a Capsule Corp time machine to warn the Z-Fighters about a pair of lethal cybernetic threats created by the rogue scientist Dr. Gero.
