PROJECT OVERVIEW:
Sonic Adventure 2 follows two distinct narrative perspectives: the Hero side and the Dark side. The conflict centers around the legacy of Professor Gerald Robotnik and his ultimate creation, Shadow the Hedgehog, sealed away on Space Colony ARK fifty years prior.

KEY CHARACTER IDENTITIES:
- Sonic the Hedgehog: A heroic blue hedgehog framed by G.U.N. forces for a high-profile chaos emerald theft. He seeks to clear his name, evade capture, and foil Eggman's space-bound plots.
- Shadow the Hedgehog: The "Ultimate Life Form" awakened by Dr. Eggman from suspension on Prison Island. He seeks vengeance against humanity following a tragic raid on the ARK fifty years ago.
- Knuckles the Echidna: Guardian of the Master Emerald, attempting to recover its scattered shards after shattering it to prevent its theft by Rouge the Bat.

CLIMACTIC RESOLUTION:
Back on the ARK, the survivors look out the window at the peaceful blue planet. Rouge comforts a grieving Knuckles, while Sonic enters the room holding Shadow's gold inhibitor ring, confirming his tragic demise following the neutralization of the Finalhazard.
